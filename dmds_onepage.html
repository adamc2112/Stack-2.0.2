Why go pro?

Wholesale delivery pricing
CUSTOM LIST OPTIONS:
Use our managed DMDS Lists and/or create custom destination lists searching by organization, contact, title, radio format, geographic location, etc.
FULL ACCESS TO DESTINATION LISTS:
Full access to the database of Radio, TV, and other promotional destinations.
COLLABORATIVE TOOLS:
Multiple user accounts within your organization allow you to share releases, assets, custom lists and reports.
EDIT RELEASES:
Send to added destinations, remove destinations, and change a sent release's content and information after it's sent - instantly updates in recipient's libraries.

Deliver your release as stream-only until the official release date to give your partners a sneak-peak and build anticipation; create a release on Friday, schedule it for delivery on Monday morning and forget all about it.
MULTIPLE NOTIFICATIONS:
Just got an add at KROQ, a 9.5 review on Pitchfork.com, or your release just became available for download? Re-notify your recipients and let them know at no extra charge!
